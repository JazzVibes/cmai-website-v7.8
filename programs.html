Programs

Every program builds balance, timing, and confidence. Classes scale in intensity so beginners learn safely and advanced students stay challenged.

Kids (7–12)

Short rounds, clear targets, and a strong safety culture. We focus on listening skills, posture, and courteous behavior while building real self‑defense basics.

  • Foundations: stances, footwork, blocks, strikes, rolls.
  • Respect & responsibility: “Osu,” lining up, and partner rules.
  • Rank steps: stripes toward each belt; testing when ready.

Teens

Stronger conditioning and problem‑solving. We connect kata to application and develop leadership through assistant‑teaching moments.

Adults

Skill‑first training that respects time and joints. We blend striking, clinch, and Small Circle Jujitsu controls with modern scenario work.

Weapons

Modern Arnis stick work and empty‑hand translations, plus traditional implements for coordination, timing, and flow.

Jujitsu

Small Circle principles—off‑balancing, joint control, and safe practice—applied to common standing grabs and holds.

Modern Arnis

Flow drills (tapi‑tapi), disarms, and empty‑hand transfers that reinforce timing and structure for striking and grappling.

Dojo etiquette & FAQs

  • Respect first: bow on/off the mat; thank your partner; light contact unless told otherwise.
  • Safety: tap early/often; coaches will stop a drill if technique or pace slips.
  • Gear: clean gi, water bottle, optional mouthguard/shin pads for teens/adults.
  • Testing: stripes mark progress; schedule posted on Events; promotion when skills are ready.
  • Parents: watch classes; we’ll give 1–2 focus items for practice at home.
