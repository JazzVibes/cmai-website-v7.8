Modern Arnis & Weapons

Our approach

Build fundamentals, then pressure‑test.

  • Basics → padwork → partner drills → controlled scenarios. Intensity scales to experience.
  • Kata to application. We extract throws, locks, and strikes (bunkai) and connect them with Small Circle Jujitsu principles.
  • Safety first. Tapping culture and clear rules keep training productive for all ages.

Rank Requirements (overview)

Full checklists are available in class. This overview helps guide practice between classes.

White → Yellow

  • Etiquette & safety (tapping, partner rules)
  • Stances: heisoku, zenkutsu, kiba
  • Footwork: step, slide, turn
  • Blocks: down, outside, rising
  • Strikes: palm, straight punch, front kick
  • Breakfalls: back & side (intro)
  • Self‑defense: wrist releases 1–3
  • Kata: Fukyugata Ichi (or dojo basic)

Yellow → Orange

  • Kata: Naihanchi 1 (structure & turns)
  • Combinations: basics 1–5
  • Breakfalls: front & roll (intro)
  • Tuite: 2 standing locks
  • Point control: targets & first‑aid rules

Orange → Green

  • Kata: Pinan/Heian 1–2
  • Padwork: jab–cross–low kick
  • Tuite: lock flows (standing)
  • Throws: 2 fundamental off‑balances
  • Modern Arnis: 12 strikes + basic disarm

Green → Purple

  • Kata: Seisan (intro) or equivalent set
  • Tuite: spiral control, two‑hand rule (SCJ)
  • Breakfalls: high fall prep
  • Scenario: push/grab responses

Purple → Brown

  • Kata: advanced set per instructor
  • Throws: hip & leg entries
  • Modern Arnis: tapi‑tapi sequences
  • Tuite: standing→ground transitions

Brown → Black

  • Kata: full review + bunkai presentation
  • Teaching: lead warm‑up + drill
  • Pressure test: controlled rounds
  • Service: assist in at least 10 classes
